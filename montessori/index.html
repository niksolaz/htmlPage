Method Montessori during a lesson

Maria Tecla Artemisia Montessori was born on August 31, 1870 in Chiaravalle, Italy.

Her educational method is in use today in some public and private schools throughout the world.

Here's a time line of Maria Montessori life

  • Born 1870
  • 1883–1896: Education
  • 1896–1901: Early career and family
  • 1901–1906: Further studies
  • 1906–1911: Casa dei Bambini and the spread of Montessori's ideas
  • 1909–1915: International recognition and growth of Montessori education
  • 1915–1939: Further development of Montessori education
  • 1939–1946: Montessori in India
  • 1946–1952: Final years
  • Montessori died of a cerebral hemorrhage on May 6, 1952 at the age of 81 in Noordwijk aan Zee, the Netherlands
